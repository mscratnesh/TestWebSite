Equity Funds

Equity funds primarily invest in stocks and aim for long-term capital growth. They are suitable for investors with a higher risk appetite.

Debt Funds

Debt funds invest in fixed-income securities like bonds and treasury bills. They are ideal for conservative investors seeking stable returns.

Hybrid Funds

Hybrid funds invest in a mix of equity and debt instruments, offering a balance of risk and return. They are suitable for moderate-risk investors.
